Sheffield Plant Club

The Steel City's premier social club for all things green!

What's on

SPC hosts regular get togethers and plant swaps!

  • Every Thursday 2pmHigh tea at the botanical gardens.
  • Every Saturday 9amGroup plant shopping! Meet in the fountain district to tour the town's many foliar fancies.
  • Third Sunday of every month, 1pmPlant exchange! Have some props or spare plants? Bring them along to Devonshire Green and swap them with our other club members!
